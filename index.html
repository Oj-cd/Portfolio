Rekindled
LAUNCHING ON STEAM · AUGUST 2026

A 2D character spell-weaving action-adventure in a fully 3D world where players combine three core elements into unique spells that interact with each other, creating dynamic combat and puzzle-solving opportunities. As a phoenix-themed mage, climb a volcanic mountain and revive the last Phoenix to rekindle the world.

My role: gameplay programmer. I architected the spell framework all six elemental spells run on, the combination system designers drive through data, interactions between spells, input and rebinding across keyboard and controllers, puzzles and interactables, performance, and Steam integration. I also contributed to enemy AI design.

How I Work

Designers ship faster around me

I build systems driven by data, so designers can tune without a code request. I measure my work by how rarely people need me after it exists.

Performance is part of the design

Object pooling, allocation control, and profiling until heavy combat holds steady. My understanding of performance comes from profiling and fixing real bottlenecks, not memorizing best practices.

The game shipping comes first

When a team lost its level designer mid production, I switched roles and delivered the levels while still writing code. Lanes matter less than launches.
